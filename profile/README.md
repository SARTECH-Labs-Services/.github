### What we build

SARTECH enforces AI policies **before** execution — not after. Our platform sits between regulated organizations and their AI tools, generating cryptographically verifiable evidence for every decision.

Where others rely on dashboards and post-hoc logs, we produce **provable, admissible proof** that governance was applied at execution time.

### Why it matters

AI adoption in law, finance, healthcare, and government is accelerating, but governance hasn't kept pace. Organizations can't prove which policy applied, why an action was permitted, or who was accountable when something goes wrong.

We make trust programmable.

### Core capabilities

- **Pre-execution policy enforcement** — deterministic rule evaluation before any AI action proceeds
- **Cryptographic evidence bundles** — Ed25519 signed, RFC 3161 timestamped, tamper-evident audit trails
- **Vendor-agnostic governance kernel** — works across AI providers without lock-in
- **Privilege-aware architecture** — built for attorney-client privilege, client-matter segregation, and ethical walls
- **Multi-tenant isolation** — designed for regulated multi-party environments from day one
